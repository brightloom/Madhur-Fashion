# Madhur Fashion Copywriting & Content Guide

## Editorial Voice & Tone
*   **Feminine:** Soft, graceful, yet confident.
*   **Aspirational:** Inspires women to feel beautiful in their own skin.
*   **Warm & Welcoming:** Relatable, size-inclusive, and service-oriented.
*   **Clear & Direct:** Avoids jargon. Describes fit, fabric, and ordering paths plainly.
*   **High-End Retail:** Feels like a premium designer label rather than a local market shop.

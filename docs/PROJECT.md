# Madhur Fashion Homepage Project Spec

## Project Overview
Madhur Fashion is an established women’s clothing store located in Chomu, Jaipur. They specialize in high-quality, comfortable, and stylish ethnic and western wear for modern Indian women. Their collection includes kurtis, short kurtis, co-ord sets, cotton outfits, western wear, printed outfits, festive clothing, and offers an inclusive sizing range from S to 5XL.

The goal of this project is to build a premium, conversion-focused, and highly responsive homepage that functions as an online flagship storefront. The experience should feel like an editorial fashion catalog rather than a generic local boutique site, drawing layout and structure inspiration from **Iconic India** while remaining uniquely branded for Madhur Fashion.

---

## Brand Positioning & Objectives
*   **Target Audience:** Modern Indian women seeking stylish, comfortable, and well-fitted daily, office, and festive wear.
*   **Unique Selling Proposition (USP):** Inclusive sizing (S to 5XL), premium quality cotton fabrics, curated trending styles, and personal customer care with easy WhatsApp ordering.
*   **Visual Persona:** Premium, feminine, warm, aspirational, and clean.
*   **Conversion Goal:** Prompt visitors to explore the seasonal collections, find their size, and initiate WhatsApp inquiries/orders or visit the physical store in Chomu.

---

## Technical Stack & Constraints
*   **Framework:** Next.js (app router) with TypeScript.
*   **Styling:** Vanilla CSS / CSS Modules (or Tailwind CSS if confirmed by user. We will use Tailwind CSS since it's the approved modern stack in the prompt if no stack exists: "Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Reusable React components, Lucide icons, Framer Motion only for subtle animations").
*   **Icons:** Lucide React.
*   **Animations:** Framer Motion (subtle micro-animations, fade-ins, and hover states).
*   **Hosting/Deployment:** Optimized for Vercel.
*   **Performance:** Mobile-first, optimized images, semantic HTML, keyboard accessible.

---

## Page Structure & Sections
1.  **Announcement Bar:** Promo banners, inclusive sizing notice (S–5XL), and quick store-hours message.
2.  **Premium Header:** Sticky navigation with logo, categorised dropdown links (New Arrivals, Kurtis, Co-ord Sets, Western Wear, Festive Wear, Plus Size), Search, Wishlist, and Cart/Inquiry bag.
3.  **Hero Section:** Editorial high-resolution model hero banner with high-impact seasonal typography and clear Call-to-Actions (CTAs).
4.  **Shop by Category:** A visually striking category grid with hover zoom effects.
5.  **New Arrivals:** Grid of product cards with interactive size selection and "Quick WhatsApp Inquiry".
6.  **Full-Width Promo Banner:** Lifestyle photo highlighting cotton comfort or festive elegance with clear conversion copy.
7.  **Trending Styles:** Showcase section focusing on Instagram favorites.
8.  **Shop the Look:** Large editorial photo paired with styled outfit components for complete styling purchase path.
9.  **Why Madhur Fashion:** Horizontal grid showcasing brand pillars (sizing, fabric quality, styling help, WhatsApp ordering).
10. **Brand Story:** A warm narrative block introducing the store's roots in Chomu and dedication to size inclusivity.
11. **Instagram Gallery:** Interactive photo grid mirroring @madhur_fashions aesthetic.
12. **Store & Contact Info:** High-priority contact section with directions, timings, and custom WhatsApp deep links.
13. **Newsletter / WhatsApp Updates signup.**
14. **Footer:** Multi-column links, contact details, store hours, and policies.

## Project Timeline & Phases
1.  **Phase 1: Research & Planning** (Analyze references, define spec/plan, create wireframe designs).
2.  **Phase 2: Documentation** (Write Project, Design, Content, and Asset specs).
3.  **Phase 3: Core Implementation** (Set up Next.js app, configure design tokens, build global layout, header, footer).
4.  **Phase 4: Component Construction** (Build reusable elements: ProductCard, CategoryCard, Banner, etc.).
5.  **Phase 5: Section Assembly** (Build out the sections of the homepage).
6.  **Phase 6: QA & Polish** (Responsiveness testing, accessibility audit, WhatsApp link verification, performance optimization).
